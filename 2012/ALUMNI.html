Founding member

I had the privilege of being Secretary General of CAMUN last year when we hosted our much less ambitious in-house conference.  CAMUN 2011 provided us with a platform to prepare for opening our doors to delegates from other schools. It also gave many of our younger delegates the opportunity to discover the thrill of an MUN conference for themselves.
Since then MUN in Cambridge has grown exponentially in numbers, confidence and experience.  This year’s Secretariat has worked tirelessly, building on what we learned last year.  Throughout this year we have cemented the things we got right, polished those things that could potentially be better and added what needed adding. All this to make sure that CAMUN 2012 would be a solid step in the upward spiral of our MUN Club’s success.
I am confident that CAMUN 2012 will display the values which make MUN in The Cambridge High School such a phenomenal success- professionalism, friendliness, teamwork, dedication and positive leadership.
May all delegates experience the things which make my colleagues and I so immensely proud of being part of CAMUN 2012.

Rikus van Eeden
